Shuang Peng

I am a M.S. student at USC, working with Prof. Somil Bansal.

From 2021 to 2022, I worked with Romi lab, as part of the HongKong PolyU. I primarily designed the hardware of a self-reconfigurable module robot.

I am widely interested in control and safety for legged robots.
