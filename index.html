Haptic hand   

status - done    Dec. 2019-Nov. 2021     Detailed info

Finger module demo   Grasp demo

A wearable haptic exoskeleton with a parallel 3DOF translate mechanism in order to deliver tactile feedback to the user’s fingertip.

A 4DOF linkage connects the user’s fingertip and back of the hand, which covers the finger’s workspace. To achieve 3-dimensional force feedback, 3 joints of the linkage are actuated by brushless motors, with high power density that delivers useable torque to the user’s finger. A grasp demo developed based on the chai3d framework.
