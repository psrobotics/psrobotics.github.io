Shuang Peng

I'm a M.S. EE student advised by Prof. Somil Bansal at SIA Lab, USC. I received my B.Eng. in Robotics from Southeast University, China. During my undergrad, I worked on haptic devices with Prof. Aiguo Song. I have also worked with Prof. David Navarro-Alarcon over a module reconfigurable robot project.

I'm broadly interested in control and safety ensurace for legged robots. I also enjoy doing hardware stuff, including design and build customized robots from scratch.
